Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

  • From what I read,Progressive Enhancement is valuing web content over presentation in a website. It involves pulling the content of a website away from the presentation.It allows you to enhance the content before putting it back with the presentation. It is a great way to improve the experience of the website. It is important in modern web developement because it provides better content and functionality to many users. Link to other article
